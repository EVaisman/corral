FAQ

Can Corral download YouTube videos?
No. Corral respects platform rules and does not bypass restrictions. YouTube links can be saved and opened in YouTube.

What can be played offline?
Offline playback is available only for supported sources (for example: podcasts via RSS and direct media files).

Does Corral require an account?
No. Corral is local-first and works without sign-in.
